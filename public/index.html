About the studio

We film weddings the way they're actually lived in.

Sangam Films started with a simple idea: ceremony footage should feel like memory, not surveillance. We shoot handheld, low-key, and close to the people in the room — the aunties directing the haldi, the nervous hands during mehandi, the half-second glance at the mandap.

Every project is edited by the same two people who shot it, start to finish. No outsourced cuts, no templated colour grades. Just your day, paced the way it actually happened.
